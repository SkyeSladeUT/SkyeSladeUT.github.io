My personal interests

I enjoy video games and movies, which has helped me get ideas throughout my college days. My favorite franchise would have to be the Legend of Zelda franchise with Breath of the Wild as my number one fav. I've also had some fun times playing the Lego games with my little brother and horror games with friends. Along with playing video games, I do enjoy creating them as well.
